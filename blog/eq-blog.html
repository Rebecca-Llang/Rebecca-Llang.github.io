What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence (EQ) is the active practice and understanding of emotions and perspectives. This involves not only understanding our own emotions and behaviours but also recognizing and empathising with the feelings and viewpoints of others. EQ is about identifying and processing our own emotions, while also being attuned to the emotions and perspectives of those around us. Applying compassion, communicating clearly, and holding oneself accountable—without becoming defensive—are all key components of emotional intelligence. These skills are cultivated and honed through continuous practice, both within ourselves and in our interactions with others.
