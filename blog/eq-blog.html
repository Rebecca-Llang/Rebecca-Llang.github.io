What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the active practice and understanding of emotions and perspectives. This is both over the self and in others. EQ is a skill in identifying and processing your own feelings and behaviours, and recognising the feelings and perspectives of others. Applying compassion, clear communication and accountability, without defensiveness, are EQ skills. These are learnt and practised, within ourselves and in interpersonal relationships.
